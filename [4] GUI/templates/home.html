About This Application

This application is designed to apply machine learning to your product!

It aims to analyze your product & user behavior and provide you with insights into how you can improve your product.

Here is a step-by-step guide that will help you to understand the process of machine learning and how it can be applied to design.

Each step will guide you through a different part of the machine learning process, from setting up your data to testing your model.

Click the 'Start' button to begin the process.
